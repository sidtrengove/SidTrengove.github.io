Findings

Western developed countries seem to have a higher percentage infected by COVID. This may mean better herd immunity or could possibly be different recordings of cases. It hardly seems likely that only 0.03% of Yemen have caught COVID especially with 5.8% being recorded in the bordering country, Oman.

In alignment with the findings above Europe has had the highest number of recorded deaths. Overall, a person picked at random from those who have caught COVID has had a 2% chance of death.
